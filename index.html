About Amawin

Amawin is a Sri Lankan Rapper, Singer-Songwriter, and Record Producer who refuses to be boxed into a single genre. Breaking into the scene with a unique sonic identity, he explores the depths of human emotion through versatile sounds.

From the raw, emotional struggle captured in "Yadham" to the laid-back, infectious vibes of "IDK" (feat. Wisher Scrappy & Dilruk), Amawin proves his ability to master different styles of Hip-hop and Trap. His music is a reflection of life—unfiltered, diverse, and constantly evolving.

Looking ahead, Amawin is set to elevate the local music landscape with high-concept, cinematic projects. With a focus on bringing an expensive, film-industry-standard aesthetic to his music videos and producing grand, large-scale musical compositions, he is on a mission to redefine what Sri Lankan music can look and sound like.

With a vision to create timeless art pieces, Amawin is just getting started.
