About

Amawin (Senithu Amawin) is an independent Sri Lankan rapper, singer-songwriter, and record producer specializing in authentic, narrative-driven hip-hop. Born in 2007, he has established a unique presence in the Sinhala rap scene by blending cinematic storytelling with high-quality music production. His creative process is defined by a hands-on approach, overseeing everything from complex lyrical compositions to the technicalities of full-scale music video production. Amawin is known for his preference for organic, acoustic-driven arrangements, consciously moving away from synthetic or regional drum patterns to create a signature sound that resonates with a global aesthetic. As a collaborator, he frequently works with artists like Wisher Scrappy and Morfyn, focusing on projects that push the boundaries of independent music in Sri Lanka. Beyond music, he manages his own digital identity and creative brand, including official merchandise and technical content creation. Currently distributed via Wave Park Music, Amawin continues to evolve as a versatile artist, merging independent hustle with professional industry standards.
